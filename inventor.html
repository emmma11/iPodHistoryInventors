The iPod Inventors

Who invented the iPod?

Steve Jobs and Steve Wozniak created the iPod in 2001.

Why was the iPod invented?

Steve Jobs wanted Apple to be like Sony because he was greatly inspired by Sony's creator Akio Morita. He liked how people could listen to music on the go!

Personal Life

When Steve Jobs was a baby, he was adopted by Clara and Paul Jobs after being put up for adoption by his biological parents. He wasn't able to uncover information until he was 27 years of age. Steve was always an intelligent and innovative thinker - even from a young age. He tested so well that the school administrators wanted to bump him up to high school; however, his parents declined. When he was older, he enrolled at Reed College in Portland, Oregon, but he didn't have a direction so he dropped out after six months.

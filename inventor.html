The iPod Inventors

Who invented the iPod?

Steve Jobs and Steve Wozniak created the iPod in 2001.

Why was the iPod invented?

Steve Jobs wanted Apple to be like Sony because he was greatly inspired by Sony's creator Akio Morita. He liked how people could listen to music on the go!

About the Inventors

Steve Jobs

Personal Life

Steve was born on February 24, 1955. When Steve Jobs was a baby, he was adopted by Clara and Paul Jobs after being put up for adoption by his biological parents. He wasn't able to uncover information until he was 27 years of age. Steve was always an intelligent and innovative thinker - even from a young age. He tested so well that the school administrators wanted to bump him up to high school; however, his parents declined. When he was older, he enrolled at Reed College in Portland, Oregon, but he didn't have a direction so he dropped out after six months.

Apple

Steve Jobs met Steve Wozniak in 1971 when he was still in high school. He was only twenty one years old. However, he was phased out and left the company in 1985. In following years, Jobs bought an animation company from George Lucas. This animation company later became Pixar Animation Studios. Steve invested 50 million dollars from his own money into the company. Pixar eventually merged with Walt Disney in 2006 which made Steve Jovs Disney's largest shareholder. Jobs returned to apple as the CEO in 1997.

Death

Steve Jobs found out he has a neuroendocrine tumour in 2003 which is rare, but doctors were able to operate. Steve avoided surgery for nine months after finding out. During those nine months, he altered his peso-vegetarian diet and weighed Eastern treatment options. In 2004, Steve finally decided to have the surgery and it was successful; however, his health problems returned in 2009 which included him needing a liver transplant. He eventually died at the age of 56 on October 5, 2011.

Steve Wozniak

Personal Life

Steve Wozniak was born on August 11, 1950 and is the son of Stephen Gary Wozniak. He took after his father with his love of electronics from a young age. Also, he was never considered a great student, but he had an aptitude for building working electronics from scratch. He also attended University of California in Burley for a short period of time. He is currently married to Janet Hill.

Apple

Wozniak met Steve Jobs through a mutual friend while in university. They worked out of a family garage for while, trying to produce user-friendly alternative to the computers being introduced at the time. Wozniak was in charge of creating and building the inventions while Jobs did marketing. These two made a great team with their different strengths.
